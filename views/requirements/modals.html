How do I change my personal information?

Adding or editing your personal information is an important step of making your account way more personal. People know who you are and it gives your profile a better personal twist. But, how do you change your personal information. Actually, it's really not that hard...

Changing your personal information is a really easy thing to do and it requires only about a minute of your time. Super quick, isn't it? When you're on your personal profile page you can easily add or edit all your personal information.

Atfter you login, you automatically get redirected to your profile page. You can also go to your profile page by clicking on the 'My Profile'-link in the top navigation, or by inserting your username in the URL. If you choose the last way, your URL should look something like this: 

https://guestbook.com/Profile/YourUsername

When you arrive at your account, you can see the small panel on the top of the right side of your wegpage. The heading of this panel says: Edit my Profile and is impossible to miss. When you click on the first link, in the middle of the page, a form will appear where you can add or edit your personal information!

At the moment you can add or edit the following things:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Date of Birth
  • Nickname
  • Country
  • E-mail
  • Bio

When you hit the blue Edit button, all your changes will save. But, there's one thing that you have to keep in mind. It is also set as a reminder on the top of the form, but whenever you make changes to your account, you will be logged out. Don't worry though, becuase when you log back in, all your changes should be visible at your profile page!

How do I change my username?

A lot of people also come to the point of where they're thinking: 'Hey, i don't like my username anymore, I want to change this'. If you're at this point to and really want to change your username, everything you have to know will be explained to you here!

If you don't like your username anymore, it's really easy to change this. However, you have to keep a few things in mind when you want to choose a new username:

  • Choose a username which describes you best. Most common usernames contain the name of the person, or a part of the name. You can also choose a username which describes what you like. Be warned about the following: When you use any kind of swear words, or words which are inappropriate, your account will be deleted. Let's just keep this an appropriate platform for users of all ages!

  • Choose a username which doesn't exist already. This sounds kind of obivous, but when you want to change your username it will alwyas be checked whether or not the username already exists on the webiste. If this is the case, you won't be able to use that username!

  • Don't use more than 25 characters. The best username is short, but strong. Try to describe yourself in as little characters as possible.

  • Just as when you're changing your personal information, you have to click the blue Edit button to save your changes. Also, when you change your username you will automatically be logged out, but don't worry. When you log back in, you can see your changed username!
